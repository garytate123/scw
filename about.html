About Us

Here at Small Charity Websites, we specialise in websites and Progressive Web Apps for small charities and non-profits organisations. We love working with charities of all sizes, but understand those small charities may not have a massive budget for their website. We believe that no matter what size budget you have, you still deserve a great website.

We are based in the North of England and would love to be involved in building, redesigning or maintaining your website. So no matter where you are in the world, free feel to get in touch anytime to talk about your website needs.

Affordable

We work with you and your budget. We don't advertise and charge a set price for our website builds because not everyone wants or needs the same features. We will work closely with you and work out what's possible with your budget. If there are some features that are outside of your budget, we will create a site scalable and ready for the new features to be added at a later date.

Honest

Your main source of income comes forms donors that are hoping that their donations are making a real difference. They want to know that you are spending their money wisely. The last thing your organisation needs is for companies to be over changing for services you don't need. We will always provide you with honest and realistic quotations for any work and hope to feel that we too are making a difference by working with you.

Reliable

We are a very reliable business and we pride ourselves on outstanding customer service. We always deliver what we promise and we aim to keep and work with our customers for the long term. We hope that our relationship with you will be a very strong one and that you feel that we understand your organisation and that hope that you will somehow feel that we are part of the team.
