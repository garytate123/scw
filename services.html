Progressive Web Apps

Would you like a mobile app for your charity or organisation but don't have the budget for it. PWA's are the answer.

Traditional organisations have needed a separate budget and teams to create websites and app for both IOS and Android. No More!

With Progressive Web Apps, your website can be created in a way that allows your site to be installed on a mobile device and benefit from some of the same features like a native mobile app.

PWA's benefit from features such as push notifications and offline usage.

Websites Maintenance

Keeping a website up to date is vital. You can no longer deploy a site and just leave it. They quickly become outdated.

Outdated websites not only put off users but they slow down and become a security risk. Images, links and pages can stop loading. The site might not look great on new devices sizes.

It doesn't take much to put users off from using your website and a maintenance plan is can be the answer in keeping your site up to date.

Most website companies will have a maintenance plan package and charge a monthly fee. this means that the price is the same for everyone no matter what their needs are. We work a little differently. We talk to you about your needs and come up with a plan together. This way, you are not being charged for services that you don't need and we too can use our time and resources more productively.
