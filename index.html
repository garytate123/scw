Welcome to Small Charity Websites

Here at Small Charity Websites, we specialise in websites for small charities, non-profits organisations, social enterprises and churches We love working with charities of all sizes, but understand those small charities may not have a massive budget for their website. We believe that no matter what size budget you have, you still deserve a great website.

We are based in the North of England and would love to be involved in building, redesigning or maintaining your website. So no matter where you are in the world, free feel to get in touch anytime to talk about your website needs.

Website Creation

Hosting & Maintenance

Keeping a website up to date is vital. You can no longer deploy a site and just leave it. Websites quickly become outdated and outdated websites not only put users off but they slow down and become a security risk. We can help with everything from domain name registration, hosting, maintenance and google analytics.
